About me


Fun facts

  What did you do before Purdue? I was a fairly mediocre undergraduate student of computer science at Lahore University of Management Sciences (LUMS) in Pakistan. With a bucket-load of luck, fairly mediocre undergraduate students should make decent college professors!

  Where did you grow up? I was born in Lahore (Pakistan), but I spent most of my childhood in West and East Africa. I stayed in Benin for the longest period of time, but lived in Ghana, Nigeria, Uganda, and Kenya for extended periods too.

  What is your favorite sport? It is considered blasphemy when you are from the sub-continent and not a die-hard fan of an obscure sport called cricket. I am no blasphemer.

  What is your favorite font? I am a sucker for the comic family of fonts. My current favorite is the comic mono.
